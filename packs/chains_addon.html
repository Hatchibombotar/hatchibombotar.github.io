This addon allows you to climb up and down chains. This allows for some really interesting builds and concepts.


Climbable Chains

This Addon Adds the Functionality of Climbable Chains. To Use it Walk Up to the chain facing forward and look up to go up and look down to go down. This uses the vanilla chain block.
